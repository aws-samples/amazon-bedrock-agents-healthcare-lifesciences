# Account Intelligence Agent — Sample Prompts

## Account Health

- Show me account health scores for all our hospital customers
- What is the health score for Riverside Community Hospital?
- Which accounts are healthy and which are at risk?
- Calculate health scores and tell me who needs attention

## Contract Renewal Risk

- Which accounts are at risk of not renewing their contracts?
- Show me all contract renewal risks sorted by urgency
- What is the renewal status for Lakewood Surgical Center?
- How much revenue is at risk from expiring contracts?

## Account Summary

- Give me a full account summary for site-002 (Riverside Community Hospital)
- Prepare me for a meeting with Memorial General Hospital — what do I need to know?
- Show me the account profile for Lakewood Surgical Center
- What's the contract and device situation at site-001?
