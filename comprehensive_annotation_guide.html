üí° Integration Strategy for Clinical Genomics

Clinical Workflow: Use ClinVar CLNSIG for immediate clinical decisions, VEP Impact for variant prioritization, and combine both for comprehensive interpretation.

Pharmacogenomics: ClinVar CLNDN field includes drug sensitivity information, complementing standard clinical significance data.

Quality Control: Leverage CLNREVSTAT, TSL, CANONICAL, and APPRIS fields to assess annotation reliability and transcript priority.
